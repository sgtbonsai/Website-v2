About Comet

Comet is a Discord bot that can do basically nothing useful, just a ton of fun features for you and your Discord server. It was founded in January 2021 under the name "Google Assistant", which then changed to "Eclipse", which is now what is current, which is "Comet".

Some of the features include:
Rock paper scissors
Murder
Harm
Basic math
